About Caitlin Collins

“A user interface is like a joke. If you have to explain it, it’s not that good.”

- Martin LeBlanc

I’m a UX/UI Designer who aims to create beautiful, cutting-edge interfaces that bring positive user experiences while increasing brand loyalty.

With a background in Architecture, I explored how users interacted with the built environment. I quickly became intrigued by the emotional responses users would have toward color, light, and way finding.

I shifted my focus from the built environment to the digital world using these crucial psychological findings to shape my designs and push me to keep researching.

When I am not busy designing, I enjoy:

  • Exploring art festivals around town
  • Trying new restaurants
  • Traveling
